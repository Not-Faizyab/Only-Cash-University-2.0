💡 Frequently Asked Questions

Payments & Refunds

How do I request a refund?
You can request a refund from your Billing Settings or email us directly at support@onlycashuniversity.com with your payment details. Make sure to include your order ID and reason for refund.
How long does it take to process?
Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days depending on your bank or payment method. You'll receive a confirmation email once initiated.
How does the Money Back Guarantee work?
If you implement the lessons and don't make any money within the guarantee period, we refund you - no questions asked. Terms apply to ensure fair use and genuine effort. You must complete at least 80% of the program and provide proof of implementation.
Can I upgrade/downgrade my plan?
Absolutely. Go to Membership Settings and choose your new plan. The difference will be auto-adjusted - no double payment required. Changes take effect immediately.

Technical & Platform Help

Platform not loading properly on mobile?
Make sure you're using Chrome or Safari on the latest version. Clear cache → Reload. Our platform is fully mobile responsive. If issues persist, try switching to desktop temporarily.
My dashboard progress bar is stuck
Refresh your page once; progress auto-syncs every few minutes. If still stuck, log out and log back in. Contact support if problem continues - we'll fix it instantly.
How to report a bug?
Go to Help → Report a Bug, describe the issue clearly with screenshots if possible. We read every submission personally and respond within 24 hours. Your feedback helps us improve!

Community & Discord Access

How to join the OCU Discord server?
Inside your dashboard → go to Community Section → "Join Discord". Click it, and your Discord will auto-sync with your OCU email. You'll get instant access to all channels.
My Discord role didn't update
Make sure your Discord account uses the same email as your OCU account. If not - message our support bot inside the server for instant verification. Role updates usually happen within 5 minutes.
